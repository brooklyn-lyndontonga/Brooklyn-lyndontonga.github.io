Ko Brooklyn LyndonTonga ahau

Kia ora my name is Brooklyn. I am a descendant of Uepohatu of Ngati Porou and Hineamaru of Ngati Hine. I currently live in the beautiful Whatatutu, Gisborne with my beautiful wildling children and amazing partner Paora Peipi, a.k.a Mr.Peipz. I lose track of how old i am, ive been 25 for 3 years, maybe 4 now. Im grateful for the opportunity to make myself known and for the opportunity to learn skills i can use to further my career into tech spaces.

Nau mai ki taku whare!! (welcome to my house!)
